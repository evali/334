Process + brief

Brief project description

Year 2015
Domain Car Maintenance
Project Type Interface design for a mobile app
Project Brief “Research, propose, design, prototype,evaluate and refine an interactive system designed for a specific domain and context”

Concept

Why we chose the domain? opportunities no dominant technology for users new to the scene a unique domain that the team was interested in learning and tackling all team members came in with a fresh mind about car maintenance which allowed for a greater learning experience especially when learning about the workflow that goes behind car maintenance Who is this for? car owners who are curious or want to be experts in maintenance of their cars Why this approach? How are we approaching this problem?

Research

we conducted user interviews to gather insights about the workflow of car maintenance interviewed experts and beginners of the car maintenance scene 3 stages - before, during, after maintenance Experience map/ journey framework
